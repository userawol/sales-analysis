Business Problem

Organizations often focus on revenue growth while overlooking profitability. High sales volumes do not necessarily translate into strong financial performance.

This project addresses the following business questions:

Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
Which customer segments are the most profitable?
Are there regions with strong sales but weak profitability?
Which categories contribute disproportionately to losses?
What operational and pricing improvements can increase profitability?
